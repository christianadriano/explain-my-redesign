Participant Profile Survey

1- What is your college education background?

2- How many years have you being writing code?

3- Do you have public projects on GitHub, GitLab, BitBucket, or similars?  Are these projects representative of your programming experience?

6- If yes, how many?

7- How many of these systems were deployed in production?

8- Have you ever needed to refactor a software system that you designed?

9- Did any of the refactoring involved applying a new design pattern?
